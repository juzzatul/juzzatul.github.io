<h1>🎯 Career Development Plan</h1>

<p class="hero-subtitle">
A strategic roadmap outlining my professional growth journey toward becoming a Network Security Analyst and future cybersecurity specialist.
</p>

<div class="card">

<h2>🚀 Career Vision</h2>

<p>
My long-term goal is to become a highly skilled cybersecurity professional specializing in network security, infrastructure protection, threat analysis, and cyber defense operations. I aspire to help organizations secure their critical systems and maintain resilient digital infrastructures against evolving cyber threats.
</p>

<p>
As technology continues to advance, cybersecurity professionals play a vital role in safeguarding data, systems, and networks. I aim to continuously develop my technical expertise while contributing to the protection of organizational assets and critical infrastructure.
</p>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>📜 Professional Certification Roadmap</h2>

<p>
Industry certifications provide structured learning pathways and globally recognized validation of technical competencies. The following certifications form my planned professional development journey:
</p>

<br>

<span class="badge">CCNA</span>
<span class="badge">Security+</span>
<span class="badge">CySA+</span>
<span class="badge">CEH</span>
<span class="badge">Linux+</span>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>📅 Career Timeline</h2>

<div class="timeline">

<div class="timeline-item">

<h3>🎓 2026 — Graduate</h3>

<p>
Successfully complete my Bachelor of Computer Science (Computer Network Security) degree while strengthening practical networking and cybersecurity skills.
</p>

</div>

<div class="timeline-item">

<h3>📚 2026–2027 — Certification Stage</h3>

<p>
Obtain CCNA certification and continue building practical cybersecurity projects to enhance technical competencies and employability.
</p>

</div>

<div class="timeline-item">

<h3>💼 2027–2029 — Industry Experience</h3>

<p>
Secure a cybersecurity-related role and gain real-world experience in network monitoring, security operations, incident handling, and infrastructure protection.
</p>

</div>

<div class="timeline-item">

<h3>🛡️ 2030+ — Professional Growth</h3>

<p>
Advance into specialized cybersecurity positions such as Network Security Analyst, Security Engineer, or Cybersecurity Specialist while pursuing advanced certifications.
</p>

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>🎯 Short-Term Goals (1–2 Years)</h2>

<ul>

<li>Complete my bachelor's degree successfully.</li>

<li>Obtain Cisco CCNA certification.</li>

<li>Strengthen Linux administration knowledge.</li>

<li>Develop additional cybersecurity portfolio projects.</li>

<li>Improve practical network troubleshooting skills.</li>

<li>Secure an internship or entry-level cybersecurity position.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>🌟 Long-Term Goals (3–5 Years)</h2>

<ul>

<li>Become a professional Network Security Analyst.</li>

<li>Earn Security+ and advanced cybersecurity certifications.</li>

<li>Gain expertise in threat detection and incident response.</li>

<li>Participate in enterprise security operations.</li>

<li>Develop advanced scripting and automation capabilities.</li>

<li>Progress toward Security Engineer or Security Architect roles.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>💡 Continuous Learning Strategy</h2>

<p>
Cybersecurity is a rapidly evolving field that requires continuous learning and adaptation. To remain effective as a cybersecurity professional, I plan to stay current with emerging threats, technologies, and industry best practices through certifications, practical projects, cybersecurity communities, online training platforms, and hands-on laboratory environments.
</p>

</div>

<div class="card">

<h2>🏆 Expected Career Outcome</h2>

<p>
By following this structured development plan, I expect to build a strong combination of academic knowledge, professional certifications, practical experience, and technical expertise. This foundation will prepare me to contribute effectively to cybersecurity teams and help organizations protect their digital assets against modern cyber threats.
</p>
