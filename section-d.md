<p class="hero-subtitle">
A collection of academic projects, certifications, and community engagement activities that demonstrate my development in cybersecurity and network security.
</p>

<div class="card">

<h2>🛡 Audio Steganography System</h2>

<img src="/assets/images/project1.jpg" class="project-image">

<p>
This project focused on developing a secure audio steganography system using Direct Sequence Spread Spectrum (DSSS) and Psychoacoustic Masking techniques. The objective was to embed confidential information into audio signals while maintaining sound quality and reducing detectability.
</p>

<p>
Through this project, I strengthened my understanding of information security, signal processing concepts, data hiding techniques, and secure communication principles.
</p>

<span class="badge">Information Security</span>
<span class="badge">Steganography</span>
<span class="badge">Signal Processing</span>
<span class="badge">Cybersecurity</span>

</div>

<h2>📡 ESP8266 & OLED Embedded Project</h2>

<img src="/assets/images/project2.jpg" class="project-image">

<p>
This project involved configuring and programming an ESP8266 microcontroller integrated with an OLED display. The system was designed to display real-time information while demonstrating embedded programming and hardware communication concepts.
</p>

<p>
The project enhanced my practical skills in IoT development, microcontroller programming, troubleshooting hardware connections, and system integration.
</p>

<span class="badge">ESP8266</span>
